# Research Library

## Georgian Wine

### Record revenues from wine and spirits exports

- **Type:** official
- **Year:** 2025
- **Cited in:** Abstract, Executive Summary, Overview, Rollout, Conclusion
- **Source:** [https://wine.gov.ge/En/News/38181](https://wine.gov.ge/En/News/38181)
- **APA:** National Wine Agency of Georgia. (2025, January 20). Record revenues from wine and spirits exports. https://wine.gov.ge/En/News/38181
